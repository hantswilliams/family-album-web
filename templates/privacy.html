Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to me. This policy explains how your data is collected, used, and handled while using The Family Album.

1. Data Collection

The Family Album collects the following data to provide its services:

  • Email address: Used for account creation, authentication, and support communication.
  • Uploaded photos: Stored securely to allow you to organize and share your memories.
  • Application usage data: Collected anonymously to improve the app's performance and user experience.

2. Data Usage

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the services offered by The Family Album. This includes:

  • Authenticating and managing your account.
  • Allowing you to upload, organize, and share photos.
  • Enhancing the app's functionality based on usage patterns.

3. Data Retention

Your data will be retained for as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide the app’s services. If you wish to delete all of your data, please email me at hantsawilliams@gmail.com with the subject line "Family App - Delete Me". I will delete your data within a reasonable time, typically within 7 business days. I am also actively working on an automated way for users to delete their data.

4. Data Security

Your data is transmitted and stored securely using industry-standard encryption. However, no system is completely secure, and I cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

5. Third-Party Services

The Family Album uses third-party services (e.g., Supabase) to store and process data. These services comply with industry standards for data security and privacy.

6. User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can request a copy of the data I have about you.
  • Correction: You can request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your data as described in the Data Retention section.

7. Updates to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app’s features or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page, and significant changes will be communicated to you via email or in-app notifications.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact me at:

  • Email: hantsawilliams@gmail.com

Thank you for trusting The Family Album with your cherished memories.
